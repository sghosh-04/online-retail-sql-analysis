# 🛒 Online Retail SQL Analytics Project

## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset using SQL to extract business insights related to revenue, customers, products, and cancellations.

---

## 🔥 Key Insights

### 📈 Revenue Trends

* Revenue shows seasonal spikes, indicating demand fluctuations during certain months.
* The UK contributes the majority of revenue, highlighting strong domestic dominance but also market concentration risk.

---

### 👥 Customer Insights

* A small percentage of customers contributes a large share of revenue.
* High-value customers exhibit both high frequency and high monetary value, making them critical for retention strategies.

---

### 🛍 Product Insights

* Certain products dominate sales volume but not revenue, indicating low-margin items.
* High-revenue products are fewer but contribute significantly to profitability.

---

### 🧠 RFM Analysis

* “Champions” generate the highest revenue and should be prioritized.
* “At Risk” customers show declining activity and need re-engagement strategies.

---

### ⚠️ Cancellation Insights

* A subset of customers has high cancellation rates, indicating potential operational or behavioral issues.
* Certain products are more frequently returned, suggesting quality or expectation mismatches.

---

## 🚀 Tools Used

* SQL Server (SSMS)
* SQL (CTEs, Window Functions, Aggregations)
